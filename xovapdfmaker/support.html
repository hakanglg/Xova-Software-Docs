Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats can I convert to PDF?

Xova PDF MAKER supports converting images, documents, and various file types into high-quality PDF files. You can combine multiple files into a single PDF document.

Is there a file size limit?

File size limits depend on your device's available storage and memory. For optimal performance, we recommend working with files under 100MB each.

Can I use the app offline?

Core PDF creation features work offline. However, some features such as ads and premium content require an internet connection.

How do I remove ads?

You can remove ads by upgrading to the premium version through the in-app purchase option. This also unlocks additional features and tools.

Where are my created PDFs saved?

Created PDFs are saved to your device's local storage. You can also share them directly via email, messaging apps, or cloud storage services from within the app.

Is my data kept private?

Your privacy is our priority. All PDF processing happens locally on your device. Please review our Privacy Policy for complete details.

How do I cancel or refund purchases?

Purchases are managed through the App Store. To request a refund, visit the App Store, go to your purchase history, and follow Apple's refund process. You can also contact us for assistance.
