Privacy Policy — TL;DR

Service: Pixarro (Xova Software)Effective: Aug 9, 2025

Pixarro is an AI-powered photo enhancement app. We aim to keep data usage minimal and transparent. Below are the essential points you and App Review need to see.

Face Data — Key facts
  • We do not collect biometric identifiers: No faceprints, no face IDs, no identity mapping.
  • Processing: User photos may contain faces which are processed solely to enhance or restore the image.
  • Storage: Photos are not stored by Pixarro. Temporary transmission to a processor (Replicate) occurs only during enhancement.
  • Sharing: Images are shared only with our processing provider, Replicate, and only for the active job.

What face data does the app collect?

None as a persistent dataset. The app may send an image that contains a face to our processor for enhancement; we do not extract or retain any biometric data.

Third-party processing (Replicate)

We use Replicate (replicate.com) for model-based image enhancement. Important details:

  • Transmission occurs over HTTPS (TLS).
  • Replicate performs the enhancement and returns the result to the device via our backend or directly, depending on your integration.
  • Neither Pixarro nor Replicate retain the image after the job completes; temporary data is deleted automatically on job completion.

Retention & Deletion

Retention policy (short):

  • Retention on Pixarro servers: 0 — we do not store user photos.
  • Retention on Replicate: temporary only — deleted automatically after processing completes (transient job data).
  • Device: Enhanced images are saved only if the user explicitly saves or shares them.

Security & privacy controls

We take standard precautions:

  • All network traffic uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • No analytics or third-party service receives face data beyond aggregated, anonymized telemetry (if enabled).
  • Users can opt out by not sending photos; uninstalling the app removes local data.

Where this is in our policy

Copy the following quote into App Review responses and into the Privacy Policy page where required:

"Pixarro may temporarily transmit user photos to our third-party processor, Replicate (replicate.com), over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection for the sole purpose of enhancing or restoring images. Neither Pixarro nor Replicate retain images or face data after processing completes; all temporary data is deleted automatically. No biometric identifiers are created, stored, or shared."

Terms & Conditions — Summary

Short version: By using Pixarro you agree that photos you submit will be processed for enhancement; Pixarro and its processors will not retain submitted images after processing. Purchases (credits) are consumable and non-restorable through the platform.

Purchases & Credits

Pixarro sells consumable credits used to perform enhancements. These are consumable in-app purchases and cannot be restored via Restore Purchases. Refunds are handled through the App Store purchase history and Apple's refund process.

Face data clause

Pixarro processes face regions only to perform image enhancement. Photos may be transmitted temporarily to Replicate and are deleted after processing. No facial recognition, identification, or biometric storage is used.

Contact & deletions

If you want us to remove any user-provided data from our systems, contact xovasoftware@gmail.com. Since we do not store submitted photos, most requests will be acknowledged and closed with confirmation.

Short FAQ

Q: Are my photos stored on your servers?
A: No. Photos are transmitted to Replicate only for enhancement and deleted after processing.

Q: Do you sell face data?
A: Never.

Q: Do I need Restore Purchases?
A: No — credits are consumable; Restore is only for non-consumable items/subscriptions.
