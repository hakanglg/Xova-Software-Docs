Privacy Policy

How Pixivo handles your photos, face data, and privacy.

1. Overview

Pixivo is an AI-powered photo enhancement application. We respect your privacy and limit data collection to what is strictly necessary for the service to function.

2. Face Data Usage

  • We do not collect biometric identifiers such as faceprints or facial recognition profiles.
  • Photos you upload may contain faces; these are processed solely to enhance or restore the image.
  • Processing is handled via secure HTTPS transmission to our third-party provider, Replicate.
  • Neither Pixivo nor Replicate store the images or extracted face data after processing.
  • All temporary data is deleted from Replicate's servers immediately after the enhancement task completes.

3. Third-Party Processing

We use Replicate for model-based image enhancement. Communication is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. No other third parties receive your photos or face data.

4. Data Retention

  • Pixivo: 0 retention — no user photos are stored.
  • Replicate: temporary retention during processing, automatic deletion after job completion.
  • On your device: enhanced images are saved only if you choose to save or share them.

5. Security

All photo transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. No analytics or marketing services receive identifiable face data. You can stop all processing by not uploading photos.

6. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact xovasoftware@gmail.com.
