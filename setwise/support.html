Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a workout plan?

Setwise uses AI to generate personalized workout plans based on your fitness goals, experience level, and available equipment. Simply set up your profile and the app will create a plan tailored to you.

Can I customize my exercises?

Yes! You can modify exercises, adjust sets and reps, swap exercises, and create your own custom routines. Setwise adapts to your preferences and keeps track of your modifications.

Does Setwise track my progress?

Setwise automatically logs your workouts and tracks your progress over time. You can view detailed statistics, personal records, and workout history to monitor your fitness journey.

Can I use Setwise offline?

Yes, once your workout plans are generated, you can access and log your workouts offline. An internet connection is only needed for AI-powered plan generation and syncing data.

Is my workout data private?

Your privacy is our priority. Workout data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties. Please review our Privacy Policy for complete details.

What types of workouts are available?

Setwise supports a wide range of workout types including strength training, HIIT, cardio, flexibility, and bodyweight exercises. New workout templates and exercises are added regularly based on user feedback.

Is Setwise suitable for beginners?

Setwise is designed for all fitness levels ‚Äî from beginners to advanced athletes. The AI adapts workout plans based on your experience level and progressively adjusts as you improve.

How do I cancel or refund purchases?

Purchases and subscriptions are managed through the App Store. To cancel a subscription or request a refund, visit the App Store, go to your subscriptions or purchase history, and follow Apple's process. You can also contact us for assistance.
